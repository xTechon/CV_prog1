## Program 1 - Affine Map

We have the following affine map:

$$
    \left [
    \begin{matrix}
        x1 \\ x2
    \end{matrix}
    \right ]
    \to
    \left [
    \begin{matrix}
        1 & 1 \\
        1 & 3
    \end{matrix}
    \right ]
    \left [
    \begin{matrix}
        x1 \\ x2
    \end{matrix}
    \right ]
    +
    \left[
    \begin{matrix}
        4 \\ 0      
    \end{matrix}
    \right ]
$$
